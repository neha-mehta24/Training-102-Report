Gynecological Diseases

The Division of Gynecologic Oncology diagnoses and treats all cancers of the female reproductive tract. In addition, the multidisciplinary team includes specialists involved in the screening and prevention of gynecologic cancers, including genetic counseling and genetic testing for individuals who are at risk for gynecological cancers.
